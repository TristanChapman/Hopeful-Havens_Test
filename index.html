About

Hopeful Havens is a nonprofit organization based in Dublin that works to combat the housing crisis Ireland is currently facing. We work to provide affordable housing for individuals and families who have become homeless due to this crisis. Many of these people have found themselves without anywhere to call home simply because they cannot afford to pay the skyrocketing rent costs throughout Ireland. If they were to try and receive help from emergency or social housing, they could sit on a waiting list for many years just hoping to be approved. At Hopeful Havens we know that waiting years to have a place to call home is too long. That is why our organization works to build new buildings to house small apartments for the homeless or renovate existing buildings into small apartments so that we can combat this issue. Our goal is that by building these apartments we can help people find a place to live faster and begin decreasing the number of people who are homeless throughout Ireland. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to have a place to call home no matter how big or small that place might be!

Services

We offer 600 square foot apartments for the homeless to live in. Each apartment includes a bedroom with one or two beds, a full bathroom, a small living area with a couch, and a small kitchen equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, stove, and oven. Residents will pay $200 a month in rent to live in the apartments, but all payments will go towards the upkeep of the existing units and towards building more apartments so that more people will have a place they can afford to live in and call home.

Volunteer

All of the labor to build our apartment buildings and renovate old buildings is done by volunteers. The first and third weekend of each month we organize large groups of volunteers to work on the construction of the apartments as well as help with upkeep of existing apartments. We are always open to volunteers at any time. To set up a separate time to volunteer or participate in weekend efforts please contact our office at 1 672 4663.
